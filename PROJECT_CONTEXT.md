# RetailHub - Social Discovery & Rating Platform

## Project Overview
RetailHub is a modern retail business platform designed for general consumers to discover, rate, and share products through social discovery and community engagement.

## Brand Identity
- **Industry**: Retail
- **Target Audience**: General Consumers
- **Style**: Modern and Professional
- **Color Palette**: Blue (trust, professionalism), complemented by vibrant accent colors

## Key Features

### 1. Rating/Voting System
- Users can rate and review products
- Star-based rating system
- Community-driven feedback
- Quality review rewards

### 2. Social Discovery
- Follow friends and influencers
- Personalized product feeds
- Community engagement
- Share and discover together

### 3. Trending Content
- Real-time trending products
- Popular reviews showcase
